Experto

Experto is a responsive app designed to be affordable and accessible expert advice from the comfort of where you are. Experto is also a leading industry tool to answer the most mundane questions and guidance for complicated projects or for when you want to learn something new. Throughout the project I was in charge of UX and UI design, content design, user research and prototyping.

My Process

Research

Research consisted in user interviews and market research. Market research allowed me to identify their major challenges. The user interviews, along with affinity mapping, were helpful to identify the relationship between many individual components, like usersâ€™ answers, observations, behaviors, and attitudes.

Ideation

I used the user personas and journey maps to create a step by step of real-life scenarios. Through this process I was able to visualize the information for the rest of the team and clients.

Wireframe

I chose to begin the low-fidelity wireframes with the sticky-note method to focus on the big picture of the design. Once the iterations were consistent I created them on Figma.

Design

Consistency is vital for Experto. The goal is for the design and layout to stay consistent throughout devices as it aids the product's usability. Users will interact with the app the same way regardless of their device.

Prototype

Throughout the process, I learned how vital user testing and challenging your assumptions are when designing a functional product. My experience conducting user-based and market research helped me identify the critical information needed for a user-centered design. However, learning different software and programs, like OptimalSort, Figma, UsabilityHub, Marvel, and WebAIM, was incredibly useful in bringing Experto to life.
